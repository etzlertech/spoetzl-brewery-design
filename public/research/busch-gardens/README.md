# Busch Gardens Williamsburg Research

**Focus:** Landscape design and visitor experience architecture from one of the world's most acclaimed theme parks
**Application:** Design principles for landscape projects

---

## Overview

Busch Gardens Williamsburg has been named "World's Most Beautiful Theme Park" for 34+ consecutive years and has won the Golden Ticket Award for Best Landscaping for 22+ consecutive years, making it an ideal case study for landscape design excellence.

---

## What You'll Find in This Research

This research collection explores how Busch Gardens Williamsburg creates and maintains landscape design excellence at a scale and intensity unmatched in the industry. The articles examine:

- **Design philosophy** that creates landscapes compelling enough to compete with roller coasters
- **Horticultural strategies** for maintaining 30,000+ plants through 6 seasonal rotations
- **Visitor experience architecture** including circulation, wayfinding, and multi-sensory immersion
- **Themed area design** across 10 authentic European villages
- **Visual references** with 150+ curated professional images and videos

---

## Key Findings

### Design Philosophy

1. **Intensity and Impact** - Landscapes must be compelling enough to compete with high-energy attractions
2. **Natural Asset Preservation** - 400+ year old trees preserved, terrain integrated into design
3. **Multi-Sensory Immersion** - Engaging all five senses (visual, auditory, olfactory, tactile, gustatory)
4. **Thematic Authenticity** - Balance of historical accuracy and theatrical interpretation
5. **Operational Excellence** - 22 full-time landscape professionals, 6 seasonal rotations per year

### Scale of Operations

- **422 acres** total park area
- **117 acres** of intensively landscaped areas
- **30,000 plants** grown in-house annually
- **60,000+ annuals** during peak summer season
- **50,000 tulips** in spring displays
- **650+ crape myrtles** pruned annually
- **10+ million lights** during Christmas Town

### Award Recognition

- "World's Most Beautiful Theme Park" - 34+ consecutive years (NAPHA)
- Golden Ticket Award for Best Landscaping - 22+ consecutive years (Amusement Today)

---

## Top 10 Design Principles

1. **Intensity Matters** - Background plantings are insufficient in high-stimulation environments
2. **Preserve Natural Assets** - Work with existing topography and mature plantings
3. **Multi-Sensory Integration** - Engage all five senses for true immersion
4. **Clear Transitions** - Physical, visual, and acoustic separation prevents theme bleed
5. **Balance Authenticity** - Historical accuracy + theatrical interpretation = accessibility
6. **Seasonal Variation** - Multiple rotations maintain freshness (6 per year at BGW)
7. **In-House Production** - Greenhouse facilities provide control and consistency
8. **Sustainability as Standard** - Environmental stewardship without compromising aesthetics
9. **Comprehensive Accessibility** - Universal design serving diverse needs
10. **Daily Excellence** - Early intervention and sustained investment required
