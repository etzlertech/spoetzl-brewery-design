## Bridges & Scenic Views

### Best Photography Locations
- **Grimm's Landung Bridge:** Crosses Rhine River under Loch Ness Monster, offers views of interlocking loops, Alpengeist's cobra roll, and Griffon's Immelmann loop
- **Germany/New France Bridge:** Provides close views of Alpengeist's first drop and vertical loop
- **High Pedestrian Bridge:** Connects to Oktoberfest across Rhine River
- **Description:** "Bridges are an integral part of Busch Gardens Williamsburg and offer up the best views the park has to offer"
- **Source:** Themetography - https://themetography.com/the-best-photo-spots-at-busch-gardens-williamsburg/

## Key Recognition & Awards

**World's Most Beautiful Theme Park**
- Named by National Amusement Park Historical Association (NAPHA) for 30+ consecutive years (some sources cite 33-35 years)
- Won Golden Ticket Award for Best Landscaping by Amusement Today for 22+ consecutive years

**Park Statistics**
- 422 acres total
- 117 acres of maintained landscaping
- 50-person horticulture team
- 14,000+ flowering bulbs planted before spring
- 50,000+ tulips in spring
- 60,000+ flowers during peak summer season
- 650+ crape myrtles maintained
- 5 major water features
- 10+ million lights during Christmas Town (largest in Virginia, one of largest in North America)
